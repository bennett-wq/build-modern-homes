## The Platform That Turns Every Vacant Lot Into a Virtual Home Listing
**Version:** 1.0 — February 2026
**Author:** Bennett / BaseModHomes
**Status:** Product Definition & Architecture
---
## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
BaseMod HomeMatch is a B2B2C platform that gives every licensed real estate agent in Michigan the ability to transform vacant land listings into fully-priced, visually rich "virtual home listings" featuring BaseModHomes modular models — and share those virtual listings with their buyers in an experience indistinguishable from browsing homes for sale on any major real estate portal.
**The core unlock:** Vacant land is the most underserved, hardest-to-sell product category in residential real estate. Buyers can't visualize what they're getting. Agents avoid listing land because conversion cycles are brutal. HomeMatch eliminates both problems simultaneously — the agent gets a tool that makes land sellable, and the buyer gets an experience that makes land feel like buying a home.
**The business model:** Free for agents. Every deal that converts through the platform results in a BaseModHomes construction contract. Agents earn their normal commission on the land transaction. BaseModHomes earns the full construction fee stack ($65K–$102K per home across dealer fees, construction management, and mortgage origination). The agent's incentive and BaseModHomes' incentive are perfectly aligned.
**The strategic impact:** This transforms BaseModHomes from a modular housing developer into a modular housing platform. Instead of acquiring every lot ourselves, we enable the entire Michigan agent population to sell our product on any viable lot in the state. Every agent becomes a distribution channel. Every search generates market intelligence. Every deal compounds our data advantage.
